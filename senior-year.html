Senior Year

This was our final year in high school orchestra. Eating lunch in the orchestra room was the most fun I had during high school because we were all good friends having a good time with each other. It was so emotional when we were all standing on the stage after Ms. Kringel gave us flowers and the orchestra played Autumn Vows for us.

  • Illusions
  • Romanian Folk Dances
    • Joc Cu Bata

    • Maruntel
  • Plink, Plank, Plunk

It was very interesting how Janae was usually first chair because of seniority our junior year but when Andy came along, he was first chair regardless of the fact that you, Albert, and I were all seniors...

Senior Regional Orchestra was so fun despite a few hiccups, but you were concertmaster! We were directed under the wonderful Jorge Aguirre. Never will we forget "last time!"

March 7, 2015

  • Of Glorious Plumage
  • Skylife
  • Simple Symphony
    • I. Boisterous Bouree
    • IV. Frolicsome Finale

Of Glorious Plumage was such a beautiful piece, especially when Andy played the solo. We were all given the chance to do so, but it was mutually agreed upon that he played it the best. Skylife was where you excelled, and where I fell short. You and Albert would often play riffs during lunch after Ms. Kringel gave you both sheet music of improv suggestions.
